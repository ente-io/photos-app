* Customizable photo grid size!

You can now change how many photos you see in a row (the option is in Settings > General > Advanced). Since this was a much requested feature we've released this initial feature asap, later we'll also add gestures

* Free up space per album

There is now an option to free up space per on device album. This provides a more granular and faster way to lighten your phone

* Remove limits on photo description

The previous 280 char limit has been increased to 5000
